# Actual Zettelkasten · Vision & Alignment

## 7. Vision & Alignment

### Truth as Infrastructure
**ID:** vision-truth-as-infrastructure  
**Family:** Vision & Alignment  
**Tagline:** Build the company on truth the way you build cities on roads.  
**Quote:** "Truth is not a report — it’s a foundation."

#### Why This Matters
When truth is an afterthought, operations drift, finance patches problems, and planning becomes guesswork. When truth is treated as infrastructure, everything else becomes easier and more reliable.

#### How It Works
- Capture truth automatically at the edge.  
- Keep it immutable as it flows into systems.  
- Make truth the base for planning, costing, and forecasting.  
- Design tools and processes around the flow of truth.

#### Reflection
When truth is infrastructure, the whole company becomes more predictable and calm.

- Truth You Can Stand On  
- Immutable Truth  
- Distributed Situational Awareness  

#### Audiences
Leadership, Engineering, Finance
