Actual Product Concepts

Show the system before the hardware exists.

These visuals are built for charter partner conversations. They make the badge, the equipment tag, and the field workflow concrete enough for an operator or owner to understand what they would actually see in the real world.

Wearable badge identity BLE tags on equipment Passive proof of work
How to use these

Lead with the field board, then show the badge and equipment tag as individual pieces. The goal is clarity, not industrial-design precision. These are concept visuals for partner alignment.

Field system view

The partner-facing story in one frame: a worker wears the badge, equipment broadcasts a tag identity, and the system creates an automatic record of where work happened and what equipment was in use.

Badge concept

A rugged, wearable identity device with a readable face and one obvious interaction. It should feel closer to jobsite hardware than a consumer gadget.

Equipment tag concept

A simple BLE identity marker mounted to mowers, trucks, trailers, or handheld equipment. The important idea is that the tag belongs to the equipment, not the property.

Concept visuals only. Intended to support charter partner outreach, early demos, and product narrative development.
