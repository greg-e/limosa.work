The Wednesday Crucifixion

A timeline using Hebrew sunset-to-sunset day reckoning and the Wednesday crucifixion chronology.

"For just as Jonah was in the belly of the huge fish for three days and three nights, so the Son of Man will be in the heart of the earth for three days and three nights."

Matthew 12:40 NET

How a Day Is Counted

Hebrew Reckoning

Evening / Night
Morning / Day

Day begins at sunset. Evening comes first, then morning (Genesis 1:5).

American Reckoning

12:00 AM / Morning
Evening / Night

Day begins at midnight. Morning comes first and evening ends the same date.
