# Hardware: Badges, Tags, and Vehicle Unit

## Badge

- Worn by each crew member.
- Logs movement and proximity events.
- Sends batched telemetry via cellular.

## Equipment Tags

- Attached to mowers, trimmers, and other assets.
- Link labor time to specific equipment and jobs.

## Vehicle Unit (ReTerminal)

- Mounted in the crew vehicle.
- Used for route view, safety documents, and crew attestation.
