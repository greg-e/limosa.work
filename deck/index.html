“Automation is good,

as long as you know

exactly where to put

the machine. ”

- Eli Goldratt Goldratt said automation is only

good if you know exactly where to

put the machine. That ’s what Actual

is doing. We ’re not trying to auto-

mate every form, every approval, or

every step of the day. We ’re putting

automation right at the constraint

— knowing with certainty who was

on site, when, and for how long.

That ’s the piece that every payroll

run, every cost report, and every

invoice depends on.

Our beliefs

We believe in flow, that effort should

go to the work, not paperwork.

We believe tools should be convivial:

they should grow agency and inter-

dependence, not extract compliance.

We believe truth should be the by-

product of work itself.

Necessary, but not sufficient

Tech doesn ’t improve flow by itself.

We put automation where it matters:

presence, time, and completion.

The team delivers,

tools reduce load.

We reduce cognitive load, so crews

can deliver the work. Truth is captured

passively, not typed.

Unhampered truth

Truth should emerge from the act of

working — no forms, no check-ins, no

manipulation.

Jobs to be done

We frame Actual by the jobs people

hire it to do: pay accurately, know the

truth on site, close the books, get the

work done.

Get paid right

“I want my paycheck to be right,

every time.” Actual captures presence

automatically, eliminating edits and

disputes.

Know the truth on site

Movement + proximity validate at-

tendance without phone check-ins.

Close the books faster

Presence streams into costing and

revenue systems so finance & ops stay

aligned.

Get the work done

We automate evidence of work — not

the work itself — reducing cognitive

drag on crews.

Product

Proof without process debt.

Effortless proof

The badge senses motion, proximity,

and duration — no taps, scans, or

check-ins.

Always connected,

never dependent.

Store-and-forward telemetry with

smart cellular uplinks.

Context without effort

People, places, and tasks are bound

by location truth — hours align with

value automatically.

Strength through connection

APIs stream verified data into pay-

roll, ERP, and scheduling — no new

portal or password.

Architecture

Integrity from capture to ledger.

Integrity by design

Each badge encrypts and transmits

events without user action. Field real-

ity becomes verifiable truth upstream.

A real-time reflection

A living digital twin links badges,

tags, and locations — a mirror that

updates itself.

Provenance of truth

Chain-of-custody from badge to

cloud so each record is trusted, audi-

table, and traceable to origin.

Outcomes

What improves when truth flows:

crew time variance down, dispatch

edits down, missed visits flagged same day.

Metrics

Confidence in every paycheck. Rec-

ognition built on proof. Flow multi-

plied.

Vision

From reactive to predictive; truth as

infrastructure.

Truth in action

Make truth effortless. Put automation

at the constraint.
