Two Outcomes from the Same Truth Layer

1. Job Activity Data — The Truth of Work

Automatic, parcel-aware records of who was where, when, and with which tools. No manual timecards. No memory bias. Just presence, motion, and proximity turned into trustworthy work sessions.

  • Verified start/stop times per worker and crew
  • Tool usage derived from BLE proximity
  • Site entry/exit tied to tax parcels and geofences
  • Immutable event stream ready for payroll and costing

2. Reverse-Engineered Schedule & Estimate — The Flow of Value

Use real field data to see how work actually flows across parcels — and use that truth to refine scheduling and estimating assumptions.

  • Planned vs. actual durations per parcel and route
  • Hidden constraints in setup, travel, and waiting time
  • Production rates grounded in real labor and equipment behavior
  • “Proof-based estimating” instead of historical guessing

How Actual Works

  1. 1. Capture at the Edge

    Badges measure presence, motion, and nearby equipment tags as crews move. Data is stored locally and uploaded in efficient batches.

  2. 2. Anchor to Parcels

    Cloud services attach each event to a tax parcel geofence, deriving WorkSessions and EquipmentSessions that reflect the real day on site.

  3. 3. Turn Truth into Outcomes

    Parcel-level truth flows into dashboards and integrations: payroll, job costing, estimating, and scheduling all share the same underlying reality.
