Actual: Zero-Touch Time & Job Tracking for Field Crews

Paper timesheets and punch edits make payroll and job costing unreliable. Actual uses a badge-based, zero-touch system to log every movement and proximity event so you get accurate data without asking crews to use phones or forms.

Learn how it works See use cases

How it works in 3 steps

  1. Badge logs movement and proximity events.
  2. Badge periodically sends encrypted data via cellular (no phones, no gateways).
  3. Platform turns events into timesheets, payroll-ready data, and job costing.
